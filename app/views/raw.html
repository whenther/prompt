Raw Data

This area is for moving your data to a different device, or backing it up. You can export a file that holds all your data, email it to your phone, and import it there to get all your stuff, ready to scroll!
Eventually prompt will implement Google Drive integration to let you move your data between devices. In the meantime, this is a good way of keeping everything in sync.

Careful! Once you import new data, your old data is wiped away!
